# Auto Product Selection Strategy Profile

This is a generic public strategy profile for creating SelectionBrief files. Customize a private local copy for your own marketplace, brand, supplier, or budget constraints.

## Default Bounds

- `marketplace`: Amazon US
- `price_band`: 25-150 USD
- `capital_style`: small team, light inventory, limited upfront risk
- `target_candidate_window`: 1-3

## Search Direction

- `scan_theme`: broad product opportunity discovery inside safe and compliant categories
- `strategic_angle`: clear use cases, review pain points, and feasible differentiation
- `why_now`: quickly decide whether an idea deserves deeper validation

## Positive Signals

- clear use case
- review complaints suggest concrete improvements
- manageable logistics
- price supports margin
- evidence is traceable
- not completely dominated by strong brands

## Negative Signals

- low price and weak margin
- heavy, fragile, regulated, or high-liability item
- requires complex certification or support
- weak or anecdotal evidence

## Report Style

- one-sentence conclusion first
- strengths and real risks for each candidate
- evidence quality
- next validation step
- elimination log for rejected sub-directions
